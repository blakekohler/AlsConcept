Q: What has been your favorite purchase from Als?

A: Easily my TF-1000 Basketball by Spalding. It's the same type of basketball I'm use to playing with in all of my leagues and its great to have one. I'm sure there's like a thousand different pick up ball games happening right now with one of those bad boys. They just wear so well and the leather continues to provide the grip you need years after the fact. Now If I could just stop losing them everywhere I go I'd be all set for life.

Q: You lose them a lot?

A: Ha! Yeah, I tend to misplace them. Well it tend to misplace every ball that I take with me to a gym. You see I start playing and when I'm done I walk out exhausted and and victorious and just completely forget I had a basketball in the first place. But I always come back and pick one up from Als. They're just so affordable and for the price your can't beat the quality. I just wish they weren't so popular so I didn't have to wait for new shipments occasionally .

Q: Do you ball often?

A: As often as possible. I recently got married and so I don't have the time I once did. But I still enjoy playing when I get the chance and having nice gear really makes a difference.

Q: What is your biggest complaint about your TF-1000?

A: It doesn't go through the hoop enough. Seriously though, the only way I'll play with a different basketball is if someone makes one that I can use to consistently hit a 30 foot fade away contested jumper.

Q: What is your next Als.com purchase?

A: Oh Easy! Golf Clubs. Im an avid golfer and I'm looking to get a complete new set sometime in the next year. They're expensive though. I'm glad that I buy from Als where I can get them for reasonable prices. I really want a TaylorMade R11S. It's got the exchange able weights in the club head to be able to customize your club swing to help you get maximum distance and try and remove slices and hooks from your game. Overall its an awesome club. But anything from Titleist,or Callaway would be just fine with me as well!
