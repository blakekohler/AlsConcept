Q: What was your last purchase from Als.com?

A: The Osprey Atmos 65 Pack! I bought the grey one and boy is it great! It's more of a backpacking pack then a hydration camp, although you can connect it to a hydration kit. I purchased it right after I got back from a trip because I knew it was time for an upgrade!

Q: What type of upgrade were you looking for?

A: Well I loved the ventalation provided by the ventilated mesh backpanel. It creates a nice air pocket to keep your back cool even when your backpacking for long distances. It's suppose to keep your back from getting sweaty and there's nothing more unconfortable then hiking miles in the hot sun with an ultra sweaty back.

Q: Is there anything else that you enjoy about your Osprey?

A: Yeah, I love the adjustable Fit-On-The-Fly hipbelt. It allow you to tighten or loosen your pack without removing it. It's super nice for when you've have long hikes where the things you've packed have moved and created an uncomfortable feeling. So to be able to adjust the pack without stopping or without taking the pack off is great!

Q: Do you backpack often?

A: Probably three to four times a summer.

Q: Whats your next purchase at Als.com?

A: I've already made it! I purchased an exped sleeping matt just the other day! It's their smallest matt measuring only three inches thick. It's pretty comfortable and has the great feature of allowing the intake valve to be adjusted to increase or decrease the firmness of the mattress.
